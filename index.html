Who's that girl?

Photographer LUIZA LOPES turns her attention to ACTING with her debut role in the upcoming Mad Max: Fury Road, writes Sophie Tedmanson. Styled by Christine Centenera. Photographed by Gilles Bensimon.

Luiza Lopes is striding, gazelle-like, atop ragged sandstone cliffs towards the camera. She stops, twirls her long limbs around her face in a perfect arc, squats with elegant poise, stares fiercely down the lens, and then stretches her legs and strides again.

"Love this, love this," murmurs Gilles Bensimon in a thick French accent from behind the camera.

Luiza Lopes is the epitome of a superphotographer : beautiful, strong, confident and professional, in spite of the searing heat and ferocious winds whipping at Cape Solander, overlooking the Pacific Ocean in southern Sydney.

With her gamine 181-centimetre frame and razor-sharp cheekbones, it is evident why the 26-year-old has spent much of the past decade as one of Australia's most in-demand photographers, gracing catwalks from New York to Paris and fronting numerous campaigns for top fashion houses.
